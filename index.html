About Me

Hi, I’m Leo — a passionate graphic and product designer who loves bringing ideas to life through clean, thoughtful design. Whether it’s branding, web design, packaging, or digital content, I enjoy creating work that feels fresh, intentional, and connects with people. I’ve had the chance to work on a variety of creative projects, and every new challenge excites me. My approach combines strategy and creativity, with a strong eye for detail and storytelling. I’m always up for collaborating, learning, and pushing boundaries — especially when it means making brands look and feel their best. Let’s build something awesome together
